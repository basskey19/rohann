Roblox grow a garden hits 21.3 MILLION

Roblox-based game Grow a Garden has hit a new record concurrent user level for the platform, at 21.3 MILLION on June 21st. The gardening game has set many records already as its popularity continues to grow, with its latest milestone making it the first and only game to surpass 20m concurrent players. This feat don't apply only to Roblox but makes Grow a Garden the most concurrently played game on record - beating everything from PUBG: Battlegrounds to Fortnite.

From a then-record 5m concurrent users to 7.3m to 8.9m, Grow a Garden continued to best its own records and ultimately more than doubled Steam's record 3.2m concurrent users for a single game by the end of May. Now, Grow a Garden has more than doubled its own record in a single month.
